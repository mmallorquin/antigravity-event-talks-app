LATEST WORLD NEWS — June 17, 2025
====================================

--- ISRAEL-IRAN CONFLICT ESCALATION ---

• Intense Military Exchange: Following significant Israeli bombardments across
  Iran — including Tehran, Isfahan, and Tabriz — Iran launched retaliatory
  strikes targeting northern Israel and Tel Aviv.

• High-Level Casualty: Israel announced the killing of Major General Ali
  Shadmani, a senior Iranian military commander, in a strike on Tehran.

• Nuclear Sites: The International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) reported
  evidence of "direct impacts" on underground enrichment halls at the Natanz
  nuclear facility, though no such damage was observed at other major sites.

• Political Rhetoric: U.S. President Donald Trump called for the unconditional
  surrender of Iran amid the ongoing war, while Iran accused the G7 of siding
  with Israel following calls for de-escalation.

--- GAZA STRIP ---

• Israeli forces were reported to have killed more than 50 Palestinians near
  aid distribution centers in southern Gaza, drawing widespread international
  condemnation.

--- GLOBAL EVENTS ---

• China-Central Asia Summit: The China-Central Asia Summit began in Astana,
  Kazakhstan, signaling deepening regional cooperation.

• G7 Summit: Reports indicated that U.S. President Donald Trump departed the
  G7 Summit early.

--- DATA PRIVACY ---

• 23andMe Fine: British authorities fined 23andMe over CAD $4.2 million
  following a joint investigation with Canada into a 2023 cyberattack that
  exposed the genetic data of millions of users.
